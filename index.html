Epicodus Track Selection Tool

Whether you're a prospective student with no coding experience or a hardened veteran about to complete the intro course, it can be difficult to figure out the course "track" you'd like to take at Epicodus. This short questionnaire should help you to figure out the best path for you!

Answer the questions below as accurately as possible


Do you, or have you enjoyed front-end (user interface) or back end (business logic) coding more?

When you style a webpage, do you spend a lot of time on the looks of individual elements, or do you prefer spend more time making overall webpage look great?

Since you enjoy front-end coding more than back-end, how much back-end would you be willing to utilize>

Would you rather work for yourself, creating your own apps/software, or for a larger (likely more stable) company?

Working for yourself can be difficult! Fortunately, you'll have skills that are applicable in both scenarios. Are you more interested in standard software development, or application (and mobile application) development?

Working for a company, large or small, is a fairly safe bet. If you had it your way, would you work on business software, mobile applications, or building websites for brands and clients?
